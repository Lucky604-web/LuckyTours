Explore Albufeira, Portugal

A stunning coastal paradise in the Algarve

Welcome to Albufeira

One of Portugal’s most popular coastal destinations, famous for its golden beaches, nightlife, and stunning sea caves.

Best Attractions in Albufeira

The Marina & Benagil Caves

Albufeira’s marina is a lively area with colorful buildings, waterfront restaurants, and boat tours. One of the most famous excursions from the marina is a trip to the Benagil Caves, an incredible sea cave with a natural skylight. The best way to visit is by boat, kayak, or paddleboard, as swimming there can be challenging due to strong currents.

Old Town & New Town Strip – The Best of Both Worlds

Albufeira has two distinct areas for nightlife:

  • Old Town: Known for its charming cobbled streets, traditional Portuguese restaurants, and live music bars, Old Town offers a relaxed yet lively evening atmosphere. Street performers and outdoor terraces make it perfect for a chilled night out.
  • New Town Strip: If you’re looking for a wild night out, the Albufeira Strip in New Town is the place to be. It’s packed with bars and clubs, hosting all-white parties, themed nights, and beach club events that go on until sunrise.
