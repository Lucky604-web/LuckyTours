Explore Zermatt, Switzerland

A breathtaking car-free mountain destination

Welcome to Zermatt

Zermatt is known for its car-free environment and the iconic Matterhorn.

Best Attractions in Zermatt

Skiing & Cable Rides to the Matterhorn

Zermatt is a world-class ski resort known for its incredible slopes and breathtaking views. Whether you're a beginner or an expert, the ski routes offer something for everyone. One of the best ways to experience Zermatt’s beauty is by taking a cable car ride to see the Matterhorn—one of the most famous mountains in the world, which even appears on Toblerone chocolate bars. The Gornergrat Railway is another fantastic way to get panoramic views of the Swiss Alps.

Zermatt’s Snowy Landscape & High Viewpoints

The town is covered in powdery snow for much of the year, making it a paradise for winter sports enthusiasts. There are multiple viewpoints, such as the Matterhorn Glacier Paradise, the highest cable car station in Europe, which offers unmatched views of the Swiss, Italian, and French Alps.

Famous Restaurants & Swiss Cuisine

Zermatt is also home to some fantastic restaurants, offering both local Swiss cuisine and international dishes. One of the most famous restaurants in Zermatt is Chez Vrony, a historic mountain restaurant that serves traditional Swiss dishes with a modern twist. Their specialties include cheese fondue, rösti (crispy Swiss-style potatoes), and locally sourced meats. The restaurant also boasts stunning views of the Matterhorn, making it a must-visit for food lovers.
